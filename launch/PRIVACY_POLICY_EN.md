# Privacy Policy — Math Game

**Last updated: 2026-05-30**

This privacy policy explains how the "Math Game" app (the "App") handles data.

## Data we collect

**1. Anonymous identifier (Firebase Authentication)**
The App assigns each device an anonymous ID to save your scores on the
leaderboard. This ID is not linked to your name, email, or phone number.

**2. Game scores (Cloud Firestore)**
We store your best scores and chosen display name (if entered) to show the
global "Top 10" leaderboard.

**3. Advertising data (Google AdMob)**
The App shows ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information and
the Advertising ID to show you relevant ads. If you do not consent to
personalized ads, contextual (non-personalized) ads are shown instead.

**4. App integrity (Firebase App Check / Play Integrity)**
We use Google services to verify that requests come from a genuine,
unmodified app (fraud protection).

## How we use data
- To operate the leaderboard
- To show ads (revenue)
- To protect against cheating and fake scores
